- **Runtime:** 30-60 seconds (7-9 shots, 5-7 lines)

## Key Elements to Include
- **Visual storytelling:** Show, don't tell - use action and atmosphere
- **Character essence:** Capture who they are through their environment and actions
- **Plot hooks:** Include GM notes with specific hooks and story opportunities
- **Tone pillars:** Seduction, deception, decay, desert isolation, moral irony
- **Phoenix geography:** Use realistic locations even when stylized
